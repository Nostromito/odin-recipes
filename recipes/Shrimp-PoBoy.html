Shrimp Po Boy

Description

A sandwich, to many people is just a sandwich, but a shrimp Po Boy is sunshine through a cloudy day. Nothing shines brighter than a smile through thick french bread, with thinly sliced tomatoes, and crispy fried shrimp in each bite. With a shower of lettuce and a remoulade you'll be glad you made, this world beater of a sandwich.

The Things You'll Need

Rémoulade Sauce:

  1. 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  2. 2 tablespoons Kikkoman Ponzu Lime
  3. 1 tablespoon horseradish
  4. 1 teaspoon pickle relish
  5. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  6. ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Sandwiches:

  1. 4 tablespoons melted butter
  2. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  3. 4 French rolls, split and hinged
  4. 2 cups vegetable oil for frying, or as needed
  5. ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  6. 2 tablespoons Creole seasoning
  7. 3 large eggs, beaten
  8. 2 cups Kikkoman Panko Bread Crumbs
  9. 2 pounds jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
  10. 2 cups shredded lettuce
